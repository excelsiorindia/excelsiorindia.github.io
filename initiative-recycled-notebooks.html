Recycled Notebooks

Our Vision

For 25 million children in India education is still a distant dream. 47 out of 100 children in class 1 reach class 8th, rest drop out majority of them being girls. The principal reason behind this calamitous situation is Poverty. Books, notebooks, stationary, is a luxury which a large section of our society cannot afford.

Excelsior INDIA has taken the initiative to make a positive impact in the lives of these children. We, being privileged enough to get good education, understand our responsibility to give back to the country by helping its future realize its full potential.

What We Do?

  • We collect waste paper from the schools, residential societies, institutes and offices which donate us waste paper.
  • The collected waste paper which is recycled and processed into high quality notebooks.
  • We distribute these notebooks in the rural areas at nominal prices.

Recycled notebooks are of high quality paper (70 GSM) which are sold at one fifth of its market price.

Besides this noble cause, our project is motivating people to make the society greener. 500 pages are made out of one fully mature tree. Hence with every 7 notebooks we make, we save a fully mature tree.

Team Excelsior INDIA believes in ‘We are One’ and will continue to broaden the scope of its activities for making a difference by improving the living standard of school children and people in general.
