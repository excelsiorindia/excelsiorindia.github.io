Education for all

About us



We are a group of IIT Delhi students. We have taken up this initiaitive to give back to the society. We believe that education should be easily accessible to all.

What we do

We collect waste paper from urban areas and recycle the paper to make notebooks.

We then give those notebooks to kids in rural areas.

We also buy stationery items in bulk and give them to the kids.

Waste paper collection

We collect waste paper from urban areas.

Waste paper recycle

We then recycle the waste paper and convert it into very fine quality paper.

Make notebooks

We then bind the pages together to make a notebook with high quality paper.

Notebook distribution

We then sell those notebooks to kids in rural areas for 3-5 INR.
